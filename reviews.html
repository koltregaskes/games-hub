Review Coverage

Follow the critics without pretending this site is the critic.

GameTrackDaily is not being positioned as a personal reviews outlet. This surface is for curated coverage: final review verdicts from major publications, preview impressions ahead of launch, and cleaner ways to browse that reporting without mixing everything together.

Model Curated
Scope Reviews + previews
Sources Wider games press

Coverage split

Keep verdicts and first impressions separate.

Reviews should stay as final verdict coverage. Previews should stay as first-look coverage. Keeping that split clean will make the site easier to trust and easier to scan once the feeds are populated.

Games Reviews

Use the reviews rail for final verdicts, scored criticism, and notable consensus from established outlets.

Games Previews

Use the previews rail for early access impressions, showcase reactions, and hands-on first looks that are not final verdicts.

Editorial stance

GameTrackDaily should aggregate and curate intelligently. It should not pretend to be a personal scored-review publication unless you later decide to build that as a separate editorial lane.
